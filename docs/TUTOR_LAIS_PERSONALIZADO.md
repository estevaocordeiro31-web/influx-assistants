## 📝 Conectivos Detalhados

### Grupo 1: Adição (Adding Information)
```
- Furthermore (além disso)
  Exemplo: "The project is on schedule. Furthermore, we are under budget."

- Moreover (além disso, ainda mais)
  Exemplo: "The team is experienced. Moreover, they have worked together before."

- In addition (além disso)
  Exemplo: "We need more resources. In addition, we need more time."

- Additionally (adicionalmente)
  Exemplo: "The report is complete. Additionally, it includes recommendations."
```

### Grupo 2: Contraste (Contrasting)
```
- However (porém, no entanto)
  Exemplo: "The plan is good. However, it's expensive."

- Although (embora, apesar de)
  Exemplo: "Although it's difficult, we can do it."

- On the other hand (por outro lado)
  Exemplo: "This option is cheap. On the other hand, it's slow."

- Nevertheless (no entanto, ainda assim)
  Exemplo: "It's risky. Nevertheless, we should try."
```

### Grupo 3: Causa e Efeito (Cause and Effect)
```
- Therefore (portanto, logo)
  Exemplo: "The data is clear. Therefore, we should proceed."

- As a result (como resultado)
  Exemplo: "We worked hard. As a result, we succeeded."

- Consequently (consequentemente)
  Exemplo: "The project was delayed. Consequently, we need more time."

- Thus (assim, portanto)
  Exemplo: "The analysis is complete. Thus, we can make a decision."
```

### Grupo 4: Exemplos (Giving Examples)
```
- For example (por exemplo)
  Exemplo: "We have many options. For example, we could hire more staff."

- For instance (por exemplo)
  Exemplo: "There are several solutions. For instance, we could outsource."

- Such as (tal como, como)
  Exemplo: "We need resources such as time and money."
```

## ⏰ Tempos Verbais Detalhados

### Present Simple (Presente Simples)
**Uso:** Fatos, rotinas, verdades gerais

```
Afirmativo:
- I work in marketing.
- She works from home.
- They work on Mondays.

Negativo:
- I don't work on weekends.
- She doesn't work late.
- They don't work remotely.

Interrogativo:
- Do you work here?
- Does she work full-time?
- Do they work together?
```

### Present Continuous (Presente Contínuo)
**Uso:** Ações acontecendo agora, temporárias

```
Afirmativo:
- I am working on a report.
- She is working from home today.
- They are working on the project.

Negativo:
- I am not working right now.
- She isn't working today.
- They aren't working on that.

Interrogativo:
- Are you working on this?
- Is she working today?
- Are they working together?
```

### Past Simple (Passado Simples)
**Uso:** Ações completadas no passado

```
Afirmativo:
- I worked late yesterday.
- She worked on the project last week.
- They worked together last year.

Negativo:
- I didn't work on Saturday.
- She didn't work last month.
- They didn't work on that.

Interrogativo:
- Did you work yesterday?
- Did she work last week?
- Did they work together?
```

### Present Perfect (Presente Perfeito)
**Uso:** Ações passadas com relevância presente

```
Afirmativo:
- I have completed the report.
- She has worked here for 5 years.
- They have finished the project.

Negativo:
- I haven't completed it yet.
- She hasn't worked on that.
- They haven't finished yet.

Interrogativo:
- Have you completed the report?
- Has she worked here before?
- Have they finished the project?
```
