## LESSON 1 (Lexical) - Friends and Acquaintances

### BEFORE CLASS - Preparação de Aula

#### Dialogue: "Catching Up"
**Personagens**: Mia e Sophia (amigas se reencontrando)

**Diálogo completo**:
- Mia: Hey, Sophia! It's been ages!
- Sophia: Mia! Oh my gosh, I can't believe it's you! How have you been?
- Mia: I've been great, thanks! Just busy with work and life, you know. What about you?
- Sophia: Same here! I actually just got back from a trip to Europe.
- Mia: Wow, that sounds amazing! Where did you go?
- Sophia: I visited Italy and France. The food was incredible, and the sights were breathtaking.
- Mia: I'm so jealous! I've always wanted to go to Italy. Did you try the pasta?
- Sophia: Of course! I think I gained a few pounds just from eating pasta every day.
- Mia: Ha! That's the best kind of weight gain. We should catch up more over coffee sometime.
- Sophia: Definitely! Let's plan something soon. It was so good to see you!
- Mia: You too, Sophia. Take care!

#### Key Phrases / Chunks

| Chunk | Equivalência | Exemplo |
|-------|--------------|---------|
| get to know | conhecer (processo) | I'd like to get to know you better |
| by sight | de vista | I know him by sight |
| get acquainted | familiarizar-se | Let's get acquainted with the new system |
| confident about | confiante sobre | She's confident about her decision |
| know someone well | conhecer bem alguém | I know her well, we've been friends for years |
| a close friend | um amigo próximo | She's a close friend of mine |
| make friends | fazer amigos | It's easy to make friends here |
| hang out with | sair com, passar tempo | I like to hang out with my colleagues |

### AFTER CLASS - Consolidation Exercises

#### Exercise 1: Fill in the Blanks
Complete with: get to know, by sight, acquaintance, down-to-earth, stuck-up

1. I don't know her well. She's just an _______.
2. He's very _______, always thinking he's better than everyone.
3. I'd like to _______ you better.
4. I know him _______, but we've never talked.
5. She's so _______, very humble and easy to talk to.

#### Exercise 2: Match the Columns
Match the personality trait with its description.

#### Exercise 3: Translation
Translate the sentences using the chunks learned.

## LESSON 2 (Comunicativa) - Friends and Acquaintances

### BEFORE CLASS - Communication Activities

#### Activity 1: Describe a Friend
Use the vocabulary to describe a close friend:
- Personality traits
- How you met
- What you like to do together

#### Activity 2: Role Play - Meeting Someone New
Practice introducing yourself and getting to know someone.

#### Activity 3: Discussion Questions
1. What qualities do you look for in a friend?
2. How do you usually make new friends?
3. Describe your best friend's personality.

### AFTER CLASS - Consolidation Exercises

#### Exercise 1: Listening Comprehension
Listen to the dialogue and answer questions.

#### Exercise 2: Speaking Practice
Record yourself describing a friend using at least 5 vocabulary words.

#### Exercise 3: Writing
Write a short paragraph about how you met your best friend.
