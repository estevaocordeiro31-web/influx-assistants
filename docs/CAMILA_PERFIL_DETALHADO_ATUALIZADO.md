### Chunks Essenciais para Restaurantes

```
Chegando:
- "Table for two, please."
- "Do you have a reservation?"
- "We'd like a table by the window."

Pedindo:
- "Can I see the menu, please?"
- "What do you recommend?"
- "I'd like to order..."
- "Can I have...?"
- "I'll have the..."

Perguntas:
- "What's in this dish?"
- "Is this spicy?"
- "Do you have vegetarian options?"
- "Can I get this without...?"

Pagando:
- "Can I have the check, please?"
- "Can we split the bill?"
- "Do you accept credit cards?"
```

### Chunks Essenciais para Cafés

```
Pedindo:
- "I'd like a cappuccino, please."
- "Can I get a latte to go?"
- "What size would you like?" - "Medium, please."
- "Room for cream?" - "Yes, please."

Personalizando:
- "Can I have it with almond milk?"
- "Extra shot of espresso, please."
- "Can I get that iced?"
- "With whipped cream, please."

Comida:
- "I'll have a croissant."
- "Do you have any pastries?"
- "Can I get this heated up?"
```

```
Barista: "Hi! What can I get for you today?"

**Praticar:**
1. Ler diálogo em voz alta 5 vezes

### Exercício 4: Planejamento de Viagem para Itália
**Objetivo:** Praticar escrita e formação de frases

**Atividade:**

**Estrutura:**
```
Day 1: Rome
- Visit the Colosseum
- Have lunch at a traditional Italian restaurant
- Try authentic pizza and gelato
- Walk around the Trevi Fountain

Day 2: Rome
- Visit the Vatican Museums
- See the Sistine Chapel
- Have dinner in Trastevere neighborhood

Day 3: Florence
- Take train from Rome to Florence
- Visit the Uffizi Gallery
- Try Florentine steak
- Walk across Ponte Vecchio
