## 💬 Diálogos Situacionais

### Diálogo 1: Lucas Falando de Hobbies 🇺🇸

> **Friend:** What do you like to do in your free time?  
> **Lucas:** Honestly, I don't have much free time, but when I do, I'm really into working out. I hit the gym like 4 times a week.  
> **Friend:** Nice! What else?  
> **Lucas:** I'm also into binge-watching shows. Currently obsessed with this new Netflix series.  
> **Friend:** Same! We should watch together sometime.  
> **Lucas:** For sure! Let's do it.

**Chunks importantes:**
- "What do you like to do...?" = O que você gosta de fazer...?
- "I'm really into..." = Eu sou muito ligado em...
- "I hit the gym" = Eu vou pra academia
- "Currently obsessed with" = Atualmente obcecado com
- "For sure!" = Com certeza!

---

### Diálogo 2: Emily Falando de Hobbies 🇬🇧

> **Friend:** What do you enjoy doing at the weekends?  
> **Emily:** Well, I'm quite keen on reading, actually. I try to finish at least one book a month.  
> **Friend:** That's lovely. Anything else?  
> **Emily:** I'm rather fond of going for walks in the countryside. There's something quite peaceful about it.  
> **Friend:** That does sound nice.  
> **Emily:** Yes, and of course, I do enjoy a good Sunday roast at the pub.

**Chunks importantes:**
- "What do you enjoy doing...?" = O que você gosta de fazer...?
- "I'm quite keen on..." = Eu gosto bastante de...
- "I'm rather fond of..." = Eu sou bem apegado a...
- "There's something quite peaceful about it" = Tem algo bem pacífico nisso
- "I do enjoy..." = Eu realmente gosto de...

---

### Diálogo 3: Aiko Falando de Hobbies 🇦🇺

> **Friend:** What do you get up to on weekends?  
> **Aiko:** Oh mate, I'm mad about the beach! I try to go surfing every weekend.  
> **Friend:** That's awesome! What else?  
> **Aiko:** I'm also into having barbies with mates. Nothing beats a good barbie on a sunny arvo.  
> **Friend:** Sounds like the life!  
> **Aiko:** Yeah, it's heaps of fun. You should come along sometime!

**Chunks importantes:**
- "What do you get up to...?" = O que você faz...?
- "I'm mad about..." = Sou louco por...
- "Having barbies with mates" = Fazer churrascos com amigos
- "Nothing beats..." = Nada supera...
- "A sunny arvo" = Uma tarde ensolarada
- "Heaps of fun" = Muito divertido
