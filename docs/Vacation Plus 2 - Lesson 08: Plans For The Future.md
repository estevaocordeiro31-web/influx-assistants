## Seção 1: Do You Remember?

**Tempo sugerido:** 10 minutos  
**Tipo:** Group work

### Itens Lexicais para Revisão

| Expressões de Circunstância | Expressões de Futuro | Advérbios e Adjetivos |
|---------------------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| (just) in case | to graduate from college | to come true |
| Well, that depends! | to graduate from high school | suddenly |
| right after | to graduate from university | lucky |
| right there | to graduate in Law | unlucky |
| right away | to graduate in Biology | fortunately |
| (I'm) out of luck | I hope to... | unfortunately |
| That depends. | I intend to... | maybe |
| it depends what... | Maybe I'll... | possibly |
| it depends (on) how... | I'll probably... | probably |
| it depends (on) where... | | urgent |
| it depends (on) whether... | | |

**Dica:** "That depends" = "It depends"
- Ex: A – Are you going out tonight? B – That depends. / It depends.

### Atividade 02: Answer Questions About Your Future

**Tempo sugerido:** 20 minutos  
**Tipo:** Work alone / Group work

Responda as perguntas sobre seu futuro:

1. What are you going to do at the end of this course?
2. Are you going to look for a (new) job this year?
3. Do you have plans to move to a new apartment or house?
4. Do you think you'll take a vacation next year?
5. Are you going to travel abroad in the next couple of years?
6. Do you think you'll ever be rich or famous? Why or why not?
7. What are your plans for the next five years?

**Exemplo de diálogo:**
> **A:** What are you going to do at the end of this course?  
> **B:** I'm going to take a vacation. And I'll probably go to Porto de Galinhas!  
> **C:** I'm not sure. I think I'll look for a job.  
> **D:** I don't know. Maybe I'll start another course.
