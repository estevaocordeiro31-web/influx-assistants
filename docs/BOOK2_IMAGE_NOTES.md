## Imagem 7: UNIT 5 - FOOD AND DRINK (1) - LESSON 9 - Key Phrases + Helping You + Dialog Samples
**Key Phrases and Expressions:**
- rather than = ao invés de
- to feel like = sentir ou estar com vontade de
- so do I / so did I = eu também
- so am I / so was I = eu também
- neither do I / neither did I = nem eu, eu também não
- neither am I / neither was I = nem eu, eu também não

**Helping You:**
- "rather than + -ing or infinitive without to"
- "Rather than eat out I prefer to cook dinner at home."
- "Rather than eating out I prefer to cook dinner at home."
- "rather than and the verb prefer: you prefer to do something rather than do something else."

**to feel like + -ing:**
- "I don't feel like eating Japanese food."

**so and neither:**
- A: "I like Chinese food." → B: "So do I."
- A: "I don't like Chinese food." → B: "Neither do I."
- A: "I'm very hungry." → B: "So am I."
- A: "I'm not hungry." → B: "Neither am I."
- so do I = I do too = me too
- neither do I = I don't either = me neither

**Dialog Samples:**
1) A: "Do you feel like eating Italian food this evening?" / B: "No, not really. Actually, I prefer Mexican food to Italian." / "Na verdade, eu prefiro comida mexicana à italiana."
2) A: "How do you like Japanese food?" / B: "I like it a lot." / A: "So do I. It's very healthy and tasty."
