## LESSON 1 (Lexical) - Friends and Acquaintances

### 1. PREPARAÇÃO (Before Class)

#### Dialogue: "Catching Up"
**Personagens**: Mia e Sophia

| Speaker | English | Portuguese |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Mia | Hey, Sophia! It's been ages! | Ei, Sophia! Faz muito tempo! |
| Sophia | Mia! Oh my gosh, I can't believe it's you! How have you been? | Mia! Meu Deus, não acredito que é você! Como você tem estado? |
| Mia | I've been great, thanks! Just busy with work and life, you know. What about you? | Tenho estado ótima, obrigada! Só ocupada com trabalho e vida, sabe. E você? |
| Sophia | Same here! I actually just got back from a trip to Europe. | O mesmo aqui! Na verdade acabei de voltar de uma viagem para a Europa. |
| Mia | Wow, that sounds amazing! Where did you go? | Uau, parece incrível! Onde você foi? |
| Sophia | I visited Italy and France. The food was incredible, and the sights were breathtaking. | Visitei a Itália e a França. A comida era incrível, e as paisagens eram de tirar o fôlego. |
| Mia | I'm so jealous! I've always wanted to go to Italy. Did you try the pasta? | Estou com tanta inveja! Sempre quis ir para a Itália. Você experimentou a massa? |
| Sophia | Of course! I think I gained a few pounds just from eating pasta every day. | Claro! Acho que ganhei alguns quilos só de comer massa todo dia. |
| Mia | Ha! That's the best kind of weight gain. We should catch up more over coffee sometime. | Ha! Esse é o melhor tipo de ganho de peso. Devemos colocar a conversa em dia tomando um café qualquer hora. |
| Sophia | Definitely! Let's plan something soon. It was so good to see you! | Com certeza! Vamos planejar algo em breve. Foi tão bom te ver! |
| Mia | You too, Sophia. Take care! | Você também, Sophia. Se cuida! |

#### Vocabulary Expansion - Personality Traits

| English | Portuguese | Example |
|---------|------------|---------|
| MEAN | Mau, malvado | She can be really mean sometimes |
| TO GET TO KNOW | Conhecer (processo) | I'd like to get to know you better |
| VAIN | Vaidoso | He's so vain, always looking in the mirror |
| STUCK-UP | Metido, arrogante | She's too stuck-up to talk to us |
| DOWN-TO-EARTH | Pé no chão | Despite being rich, he's very down-to-earth |
| ACQUAINTANCE | Conhecido (pessoa) | She's just an acquaintance, not a close friend |
| OUTGOING | Extrovertido | He's very outgoing and makes friends easily |
| SHY | Tímido | She's too shy to speak in public |
| RELIABLE | Confiável | He's very reliable, you can count on him |
| TRUSTWORTHY | Digno de confiança | She's trustworthy, I tell her everything |
| LOYAL | Leal | A loyal friend will always support you |

#### Key Phrases / Chunks

| Chunk | Equivalência | Exemplo |
|-------|--------------|---------|
| get to know | conhecer (processo) | I'd like to get to know you better |
| by sight | de vista | I know him by sight, but we've never talked |
| get acquainted | familiarizar-se | Let's get acquainted with the new system |
| confident about | confiante sobre | She's confident about her decision |
| know someone well | conhecer bem alguém | I know her well, we've been friends for years |
| a close friend | um amigo próximo | She's a close friend of mine |
| make friends | fazer amigos | It's easy to make friends here |
| hang out with | sair com, passar tempo | I like to hang out with my colleagues |
| catch up | colocar a conversa em dia | We should catch up over coffee |
| it's been ages | faz muito tempo | It's been ages since I saw you! |
| take care | cuide-se | Take care! See you soon! |

### 2. AULA NA ESCOLA (Lesson)

#### Atividades de Prática:
1. **Vocabulary Drill**: Praticar pronúncia e significado
2. **Pair Work**: Diálogos usando os chunks
3. **Role Play**: Simular reencontro com amigo
4. **Discussion**: Descrever personalidade de amigos

### 3. CONSOLIDAÇÃO (After Class)

#### Exercise 1: Fill in the Blanks
Complete with: get to know, by sight, acquaintance, down-to-earth, stuck-up

1. I don't know her well. She's just an _______.
2. He's very _______, always thinking he's better than everyone.
3. I'd like to _______ you better.
4. I know him _______, but we've never talked.
5. She's so _______, very humble and easy to talk to.

**Answer Key**: 1. acquaintance, 2. stuck-up, 3. get to know, 4. by sight, 5. down-to-earth

#### Exercise 2: Match the Columns
Match the personality trait with its description.

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 1. Mean | a. Someone who is easy to talk to |
| 2. Vain | b. Someone who is unkind |
| 3. Outgoing | c. Someone who cares too much about appearance |
| 4. Shy | d. Someone who is friendly and social |
| 5. Down-to-earth | e. Someone who is quiet and reserved |

**Answer Key**: 1-b, 2-c, 3-d, 4-e, 5-a

#### Exercise 3: Translation
Translate using the chunks learned:

1. Eu gostaria de te conhecer melhor.
2. Eu o conheço de vista.
3. Ela é uma amiga próxima.
4. Vamos colocar a conversa em dia!
5. Faz muito tempo que não te vejo!

**Answer Key**:
1. I'd like to get to know you better.
2. I know him by sight.
3. She's a close friend.
4. Let's catch up!
5. It's been ages since I saw you!

## LESSON 2 (Comunicativa) - Friends and Acquaintances

### 1. PREPARAÇÃO (Before Class)

#### Communication Activity 1: Describe a Friend
Use the vocabulary to describe a close friend:
- What's their personality like?
- How did you meet?
- What do you like to do together?

#### Communication Activity 2: Role Play - Meeting Someone New
Practice introducing yourself and getting to know someone at:
- A party
- A new job
- A conference

#### Communication Activity 3: Discussion Questions
1. What qualities do you look for in a friend?
2. How do you usually make new friends?
3. Describe your best friend's personality.
4. Do you prefer having many acquaintances or a few close friends?

### 2. AULA NA ESCOLA (Lesson)

#### Atividades Comunicativas:
1. **Group Discussion**: Share about friends and friendships
2. **Information Gap**: Discover information about classmates
3. **Presentation**: Present your best friend to the class
4. **Debate**: Quality vs. quantity in friendships

### 3. CONSOLIDAÇÃO (After Class)

#### Exercise 1: Listening Comprehension
Listen to the dialogue and answer:
1. Where did Sophia travel to?
2. What did she eat every day?
3. What do Mia and Sophia plan to do?

#### Exercise 2: Speaking Practice
Record yourself:
1. Describing a friend using at least 5 vocabulary words
2. Having a conversation about catching up with a friend

#### Exercise 3: Writing
Write a short paragraph (50-100 words) about:
- How you met your best friend
- What makes them special
- A memorable experience together
