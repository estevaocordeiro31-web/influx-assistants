## Seção 2: Putting Into Practice

**Tempo sugerido:** 10 minutos  
**Tipo:** Pair work

### Atividade: Complete the Conversations

Complete os diálogos com as expressões do banco de palavras:

**Banco de palavras:** listen to music, What kind of music, three times a week, once a week, not really, play checkers, play chess, Which sports, how often, a lot of fun, What do you like to do

#### Diálogo 1: Música
> **A:** What do you like to do in your free time?  
> **B:** I like to relax and listen to music.  
> **A:** What kind of music do you like?  
> **B:** Oh, I really like soft music.

#### Diálogo 2: Esportes
> **A:** Which sports do you like to play?  
> **B:** I like to play soccer and tennis.  
> **A:** And how often do you play them?  
> **B:** I play soccer three times a week and tennis once a week.

#### Diálogo 3: Jogos
> **A:** Do you like to play chess?  
> **B:** No, not really, but I think playing cards is a lot of fun.  
> **A:** What else do you like to play?  
> **B:** I kind of like to play checkers, but it's not my favorite game.

## Seção 4: Boost Your Vocabulary!

**Tempo sugerido:** 10 minutos  
**Tipo:** Group work

### Vocabulário Extra sobre Tempo Livre

| English | Português |
|---------|-----------|
| What are your hobbies? | Quais são os seus hobbies? |
| I don't have much time for hobbies. | Não tenho muito tempo para hobbies. |
| I'm into... | Eu gosto de... / Eu curto... |
| I'm keen on... | Eu gosto de... / Sou aficionado... |
| I'm fond of... | Eu gosto de... / Tenho carinho... |

**Dicas para os alunos:**
- "Keen on" é mais comum no inglês britânico
  - Ex: I'm not very keen on basketball. / Tom is very keen on Anna.
- "Fond of" é comum tanto no inglês britânico quanto americano
  - Ex: Joe is quite fond of her. / She's not very fond of cooking.
- "Enjoy + do(ing)": Do you enjoy playing cards?
