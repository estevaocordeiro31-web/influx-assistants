## 1. COMMUNICATION ACTIVITIES (Before Class) - Páginas 148-149

### EXERCISE 1 - Listen and Repeat Dialogs
**Instrução**: Listen and repeat the first part of the following dialogs and then you'll hear the respective reply.

| # | Speaker A | Speaker B |
|---|-----------|-----------|
| 1 | How can she be so ungrateful after all I've done for her? | The problem is that she can't help envying your success. |
| 2 | I've been trying, so far without success, to convince him to take part in the competition. | Well, he's a good player, but has very little confidence in himself. |
| 3 | Say, how did Paul's job interview go? | I believe the interview went smoothly, because he came out of it feeling pretty confident. |
| 4 | Oh, gee, you've just knocked over my glass of wine! | Oh, how clumsy of me. I'm terribly sorry. |
| 5 | I don't need any advice from you so leave me alone. | How can you be so mean and unfair? I was only trying to help. |
| 6 | That girl, Sarah, is she your girlfriend or something? | No, she's just an acquaintance of mine, that's all. |
