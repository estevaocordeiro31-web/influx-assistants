# Vacation Plus 2 - Lesson 01: Going on Vacation

## Informações da Lição

- **Livro:** Vacation Plus 2
- **Lição:** 01
- **Tema:** Going on Vacation (Indo de Férias)
- **Duração Total:** ~50 minutos (1 hora de aula)

## Objetivos da Lição

- Talking about your vacation (Falar sobre suas férias)
- Asking about your friends' vacation (Perguntar sobre as férias dos amigos)

---

## Seção 1: Do You Remember?

**Tempo sugerido:** 10 minutos  
**Tipo:** Group work

### Itens Lexicais para Revisão

| Expressões de Viagem | Perguntas e Respostas | Expressões de Tempo/Transporte |
|---------------------|----------------------|-------------------------------|
| to take a picture | It was a lot of fun! | most of the time |
| to go on a trip | What was the weather like? | at least |
| to take a trip | What's it like there? | as usual |
| to take a trip abroad | How did you like it there? | to spend your vacation |
| to have a picnic | How long were you there? | by car |
| to go fishing | for about [three weeks] | by bus |
| to go camping | How are you doing? | by plane |
| to go sightseeing | Not bad. | by train |
| How was your vacation? | Pretty well. | every single day |
| How was your trip? | all the time | I can't believe it's over. |

**Instruções para o instrutor:**
- Go through the lexical items (words, expressions, and sentences) with students
- Try to give examples on how to use the lexical items; ask questions using them
- Only if necessary, give the Portuguese equivalent
- The aim here is to show students what they already know
- Try to be as fun as possible when going over the items and encourage learners to give examples and ask questions as well

## Seção 2: Putting Into Practice

**Tempo sugerido:** 10 minutos  
**Tipo:** Pair work

### Diálogo para Completar

Complete o diálogo usando as expressões do "Do You Remember?":

| Personagem | Fala |
|-----------|------|
| **Dennis** | Hi there, _how are you doing_? |
| **Suellen** | _Pretty well!_ How about you? |
| **Dennis** | _Not bad_. Say, _how was your vacation_? |
| **Suellen** | Oh, it was awesome! _I took a trip abroad_ and really enjoyed it. |
| **Dennis** | No kidding!? Where did you travel to? |
| **Suellen** | I went to Stockholm, Sweden. |
| **Dennis** | How nice! And _how long were you there_? |
| **Suellen** | I was there _for about a month_. |
| **Dennis** | And _how did you like_ Stockholm? |
| **Suellen** | Oh, I really enjoyed it! It's probably the most beautiful city I've ever been to! |
| **Dennis** | And tell me, _what was the weather like_ there? |
| **Suellen** | Oh, it was great! It was sunny _most of the time_. |
| **Dennis** | Did you go out a lot there? |
| **Suellen** | I sure did! |

**Instruções para o instrutor:**
- Have students form pairs
- Explain the activity to them
- After a few minutes, check their answers
- Then, ask them to practice the dialog with each other
- Circulate to listen for pronunciation and intonation
- Help pairs if necessary!

## Seção 3: Vacation & Weather Puzzle

**Tempo sugerido:** 10 minutos  
**Tipo:** Pair work / Group work

### Palavras Cruzadas

| # | Dica | Resposta |
|---|------|----------|
| 1 | The opposite of light rain is _______ rain. | HEAVY |
| 2 | Document that you have to show when you enter or leave a country. | PASSPORT |
| 3 | Large building with thick walls and towers, built in the past by kings and queens or other important people. | CASTLE |
| 4 | People usually travel by _______ to long-distance trips. | PLANE |
| 5 | _______ is a synonym of great, very good. E.g. That's a _______ (=great) idea! | TERRIFIC |
| 6 | Piece of clothing worn for swimming. | SWIMSUIT |
| 7 | The weather is _______ when there's fog. | FOGGY |
| 8 | The weather is snowy when there's a lot of _______. | SNOW |
| 9 | It's too cold outside! Here, put on your nice _______ coat. | WARM |
| 10 | Place where people stay, usually for a short time, paying for their rooms and meals, when traveling. | HOTEL |
| 11 | When you travel to a foreign country you travel _______. | ABROAD |
| 12 | The same as photograph. | PICTURE |
| 13 | There are lots of tourists sunbathing on the _______. | BEACH |
| 14 | When you travel, if you want to visit famous and interesting places you go _______. | SIGHTSEEING |
| 15 | Did you enjoy your _______ (viagem) to Disneyland? | TRIP |

**Instruções para o instrutor:**
- Explain the activity to students
- Students solve the puzzle in pairs
- Help pairs if necessary
- After a couple of minutes, ask a few students to share their answers with the class
- You may turn the activity into a competition, depending on the students

## Seção 4: Now, It's Your Turn!

**Tempo sugerido:** 20 minutos  
**Tipo:** Group work

### Atividade de Conversação

**Tema:** Tell your classmates about a recent short trip you've taken.

**Perguntas guia para a conversa:**
- Who did you go with?
- When was it?
- What was the weather like?
- Did you enjoy it?
- How long did it take you to get there?
- Where did you stay?
- Did you go there by bus?
- What was the food like?
- Did you stay in a hotel?
- How did you like it there?

**Exemplo de resposta:**
> "I had a terrific weekend at the beach..."

**Instruções para o instrutor:**
- Explain the activity to students
- You're going to lead a conversation about recent short trips students have taken
- Help students get started, if necessary
- One student talks about the trip and the others ask follow up questions such as the ones they have in their books
