## Imagem 2: Useful Phrases - When you are at school (Página 1)
**Greetings:**
- hi / hello / how are you?
- excuse me

**Goodbyes:**
- bye / goodbye / see you / see you next class

**When you don't understand:**
- excuse me? / sorry? / pardon?
- can you repeat, please?
- sorry, I didn't understand

**Asking permission:**
- can I go to the restroom, please?
- can I open the door?
- can I close the window?

**When you have a question:**
- what's this in English?
- how do you say... in English?
- how do you pronounce this word?

## Imagem 3: Useful Phrases - Usually used by your teachers (Página 2)
**Teacher Instructions:**
- please, close your books / open your books
- go to the board / write... on the board
- repeat after me / one at a time

**Motivating:**
- great / excellent / very good
- that's right / it's alright / it's OK
- come on, you can do it
