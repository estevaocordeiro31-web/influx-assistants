## Seção 2: Putting Into Practice

**Tempo sugerido:** 15 minutos  
**Tipo:** Pair work

### Atividade 01: Match the Phrases

Combine as frases das colunas para formar sentenças completas:

| Início | Resposta | Final |
|--------|----------|-------|
| a) I don't think you... | (c) | to her mother about her school grades. |
| b) It's not a good idea to make... | (g) | my advice. |
| c) She lied... | (f) | what he thinks. |
| d) How about... | (b) | jokes about others. |
| e) I think you should walk... | (a) | should lie to your parents. |
| f) I don't care... | (e) | instead of going by car. |
| g) I really think you should take... | (k) | with your suggestion. |
| h) Once again I don't agree... | (d) | taking a break now? |

**Gabarito:** C, G, F, B, A, E, H, D

## Seção 4: Boost Your Vocabulary!

**Tempo sugerido:** 10 minutos  
**Tipo:** Group work

### Vocabulário Extra sobre Conselhos e Sugestões

| English | Português |
|---------|-----------|
| Tell someone how you feel. | Diga a alguém como se sente. |
| Accept the situation. | Aceite a situação. |
| What I always do is... | O que eu sempre faço é... |
| a word of advice | um conselho |
| Can I ask your advice? | Posso pedir seu conselho? |
| I want to ask your advice about... | Quero pedir seu conselho sobre... |
| I advise you to... | Eu te aconselho a... |

**Dica:** "A word of advice" = "a piece of advice"
- Ex: A word of advice: tell her the truth.
- Ex: Let me give you a piece of advice: don't do that!
